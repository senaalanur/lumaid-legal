Privacy Policy

Your privacy
matters to us.

Last updated May 21, 2026

Lumaid is a wellness companion app. We collect only what's necessary to make the app work and improve your experience. We never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Lumaid, we may collect:

  • Account information — your email address when you create an account via Supabase authentication.
  • Mood data — daily mood check-ins you log within the app, stored locally on your device.
  • Conversation data — messages you send to AI companions, processed in real-time to generate responses. Conversation summaries may be stored to enable memory features.
  • Usage data — basic app usage statistics such as streak counts and message counts, stored locally on your device.
  • Newsletter preference — if you opt in, your email address for founder updates.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the AI companion experience
  • To enable persistent memory so your companion remembers context across sessions
  • To send you streak reminders and wellness notifications (only if you grant permission)
  • To send founder updates and app news (only if you opt in)
  • To maintain your account and authenticate you securely

3. AI & Third-Party Services

Lumaid uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Groq API (LLaMA 3.3 70B) — processes your messages to generate AI responses. Messages are sent to Groq's servers for inference. Please review Groq's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — provides authentication and database storage for your account and memory data. Please review Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — hosts our backend server that proxies API requests. No personal data is permanently stored on Railway.

We never expose your API keys or credentials to third parties beyond what's necessary to operate the service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Most of your data (mood history, chat history, streak data) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly required for a feature (such as persistent AI memory).

Account data is stored securely in Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled — meaning only you can access your own data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of newsletter communications at any time via Profile settings
  • Use Lumaid as a guest without creating an account

To delete your account, go to Profile → Delete Account within the app. This permanently removes all your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Lumaid is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email if you have an account.
